  • Fuel Your Body Right. Nutrition is the foundation of any transformation.

Workout Techniques

Strength Training

  • Compound Movements: Exercises that target multiple muscle groups (e.g., squats, deadlifts).
  • Progressive Overload: Gradually increasing weight or resistance to build strength.
  • Supersets: Performing two exercises back-to-back with little rest.

Nutrition

  • Pre-Workout Fuel: Consuming carbs and protein to energize workouts.
  • Post-Workout Recovery: Eating protein and carbs to aid muscle recovery.
